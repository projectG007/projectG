Still Us

I don’t know when it happened —
but somewhere between all the laughs, late-night talks,
and quiet moments… you became home.

Loving you feels effortless,
like my heart just knows where it belongs.

No big words, no promises wrapped in gold —
just you and me,
choosing each other every day.

Happy Anniversary, my love.
Here’s to everything we are,
and everything we’ll become.
